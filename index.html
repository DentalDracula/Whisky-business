Cakes
The first kind of cakes were made by mixing wgite flour, egg, butter and table sugar. Now there is a huge variety of cakes with some classic flavours like black forest, chocolate and few weird like sweet potato and beetroot.
Bread
Bread is a staple food for many people since many centuaries. Every region in the world has some kind of bread special to that region. In its most simple form it is preprared from flour and water. It is the oldest human made food.
Pies
Pies are perfect way to start your making adventure. The crust can be of anykind and the fillings are usually made out of a fruit compote. They are one of the easier recipe. Apple pie is famous for its classic autumn flavours.
Cookies
Cookies are a small, flat, sweet, baked good, usually containing flour, eggs, sugar, and either butter, cooking oil or another oil or fat. A good cookie melts in your mouth while having that perfect crumblieness. Cookies are comfort food.
Brownie
A brownie isa flat, baked dessert square that was developed in the United States at the end of the 19th century. Unlike cake they are not fluffly but are fudgy and dense. Usually made out of coco powder, new varieties like blondie are also popluar.
Cheescake
Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest, layer consists of a mixture of a soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar. If there is a bottom layer, it most often consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, or sponge cake.
